Our story

One cart. One dream. The ONE.

Started by Bharath M G and friends in 2025, The ONE began as a weekend dream on Chitlapakkam Main Road — a mint-green cart, a hot waffle iron, and brownies baked in small batches with real chocolate and country sugar.

One year and thousands of happy bites later, the Saturday-evening queue says it better than we ever could. We're only open on weekends — because good things are worth waiting for.
